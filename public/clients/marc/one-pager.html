Three things worth experimenting with
Move 01
Keep the casual voice. Layer in depth.
Your in-the-moment posting is part of why the comment section is so warm - keep it. Two small dials: bring weekly cadence from 4.2 down to ~3 posts, and add 1-2 deeper, more thoughtful posts a month. The mix lifts your per-post average and pulls the right audience in.
Move 02
Lead with takes on cultural moments.
What breaks through across the field is short, opinionated takes on cultural and industry moments - not product announcements. Your Figure 03 post ("Impressive.") is exactly the shape. Lean into perspectives on what the world is already watching.
Move 03
Reply in your own comment section.
Pichai replied to 25 of his own comment threads in this window - more than any peer - and runs the highest engagement rate and the second-highest comment sentiment in the set. Even a few replies a week to the founders who already show up for you would compound fast.
