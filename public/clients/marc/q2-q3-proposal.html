Two Moments Per Month

A moment is a single, crafted post built around something that matters — an earnings release, a product launch, a company milestone, a narrative Marc wants to own.

A great moment doesn't just perform well on its own. It pulls in a new audience — investors, founders, journalists, operators — who stay. They follow Marc, they engage with the regular content, they pay closer attention next time. Each moment raises the baseline. Over two quarters, that compounds into something real: Marc becoming one of the most thoughtful and impactful voices in enterprise tech, with an audience that reflects that.

The impact of a moment depends on two things: the quality of the post itself and the orchestration around it. We own both.

How we build a moment

  • Source: On our weekly calls, you surface what's coming — launches, announcements, data points, cultural moments worth capturing.
  • Build: We take ownership. We pull the assets, find the angle, write the narrative, and shape it into something that reads like Marc at his best.
  • Approve: Every moment goes through the internal approval flow before anything goes live. Comms, legal, whoever needs eyes.
  • Amplify: Each moment gets a tailored push strategy — corporate handles coordinated to quote-post at the right time, C-suite posts that reinforce the narrative, Slack activation to rally employees, and content drafted for specific handles most relevant to the moment. The mix changes each time. We figure out what fits and run it.

What Makes Each Moment Better

Everything between moments exists to make the next one land harder.

Advisory — keeping the regular content sharp

We review and advise on everything going out on Marc's social, not just the moments we build. Recommendations on voice, cadence, engagement strategy. Flagging conversations worth jumping into (1-2x/month). Competitor monitoring — who's gaining traction, what's working. On X, curated lists so you never miss what matters in Marc's ecosystem.

On LinkedIn, we're hands-on — we already have access, we review posts before they go out, and we're shaping voice and content themes. On X, our role is advisory until access opens up — we provide the strategy and recommendations, and as access grows, so does our involvement.

This matters for moments because the stronger the regular content is, the more runway a moment has. A spike reaches further when the baseline is already high.

Analytics — closing the loop

We layer on top of Sprout and Dignitas and pull data continuously — not just at the end of the quarter. What's working, what's not, what the platforms are rewarding, how competitors are moving. This feeds directly into how we build the next moment.

Once per quarter, we formalize everything into a comprehensive report with a 30-minute walkthrough:

  • Moment analysis: What drove engagement, what to repeat, what to change
  • Benchmarking: Marc's performance vs. historical trends, competitors, and our proprietary dataset of 10K+ influential profiles
  • Trend synthesis: What content types are resonating, platform shifts, competitor moves
  • Recommendations: Not just what happened — what to do next

The quarterly report closes the loop. The ongoing work is what keeps it tight in between.
