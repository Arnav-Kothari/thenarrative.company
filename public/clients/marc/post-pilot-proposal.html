Workstream 03

Amplification - Standardized Pipeline

Amplification becomes a checklist, not a fire drill. Every moment ships with a pre-built cascade - exec engagement, corporate handles, and internal activation all coordinated before publish.

Executive Amplification
  • 3-4 execs locked in for every moment - a consistent core group so we can go deeper in their comms cycles
  • Direct comms relationships - we coordinate directly with their teams, not through layers
  • Exec amplification leans LinkedIn - strongest platform, most control
  • Mutual value: Marc reposts their content too - the pitch is that this benefits both sides
Corporate + Product Handles
  • Ad hoc per moment - we coordinate corporate handles when the moment calls for it
  • Corporate handles lean X - where brand accounts have the most reach
  • Slack activation on every moment - organic employee engagement

Workstream 04

Analytics - Competitive Intelligence

Expanded competitive analysis, continuous insights fed back in real time, and a quarterly report that shows the impact of the work.

  • Custom-built intelligence layer: A proprietary dataset of the 10,000 most influential profiles in the ecosystem - live, filtered, and continuously updated
  • Expanded competitive set: Bigger batch of CEO and corporate handles for benchmarking
  • Continuous insights: Post length, timing, and topic recommendations fed back in real time
  • Quarterly report + walkthrough: Moment analysis, benchmarking, trends, and forward-looking recommendations
  • Shared data: Reports shared with the team - and eventually with Marc
