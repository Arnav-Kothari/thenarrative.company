Strategic Recommendations

Seven things to change now

Based on competitive patterns, audience signals, and content performance data.

01
Cut Volume in Half
Go from 11/week to 5-6/week. Every post should pass a simple test: "Would a human stop scrolling for this?" Fewer, higher-quality posts will lift aggregate ER and reduce content fatigue.
02
Develop a Distinctive Voice
Drop title case and em dashes. Lead with insight, not product specs. Adopt a "reply-to-timeline" format — posts that feel like they belong in a conversation, not a press kit.
03
Diversify Away from Agentforce
Customer results with specifics ("OpenTable cut resolution time by 40%"), cultural hooks (F1 AI Fan Companion hit 107K views), and Marc Benioff's CEO voice (79K avg views) all outperform product pushes.
04
Fix the Comment Section
Engage as a personality, not a support bot. Reduce self-reply threads. Address public complaints with real, human responses. HubSpot's 19.2% positive comment rate is the benchmark.
05
Adopt the Short + Media Formula
Posts under 100 characters with attached media drive the highest ER across every competitor. Ask more questions — ServiceNow gets 1.34% ER with question-based posts vs. Salesforce's 12.1% question rate.
06
Build Rapid-Response Muscle
The MrBeast campaign proved Salesforce can generate cultural conversation. Build the operational capacity to react to industry news, cultural moments, and platform trends within hours, not weeks.
07
Lean Into Marc Benioff
CEO posts average 79K views. The personal brand has gravitational pull. Amplify, cross-post, and build content that bridges the CEO voice with the brand account to capture engaged followers.

Salesforce has the largest platform on X.
